Help & FAQ

Which Mode should I use?

  • Tonal CR (Beeps): Best for "Ringing" tinnitus. It actively desynchronizes neurons using random patterns.
  • Notched Noise (Static): Best for "Hissing" tinnitus or if the beeps annoy you. It plays soothing white noise with your pitch removed to rest the brain.

Instructions

  1. Match Pitch: Use the slider to find the pitch that matches your ringing.
  2. Low Volume: Set volume so it is barely audible.
  3. Consistency: Try 15-30 minutes, twice a day.

Strange Sounds (Ghost Effects)

You may experience auditory illusions. These are signs of neuroplasticity:

  • "Scrambling": Tones sound chaotic. Your brain is failing to find a pattern (this is the goal!).
  • "The Gallop": Tones seem to speed up. This is your brain trying to organize the sound.
